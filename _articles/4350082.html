Sharing a group lets you securely share logins, notes, documents, and more with family or team members—while keeping full control over access.

Here’s how to share a group:

  1. Open the Password Manager app.

  2. Find the Group you want to share.

    • Tap and hold the group, then select Manage, or

    • Open the group and tap its name at the top to access its details.

  3. Tap Add Member.
    A scanner window will appear, allowing you to:

    • Scan the new member’s QR code, or

    • Tap Photo or File to import a saved QR code from your device.

  4. Tap Done in the top right corner to save the changes.

❗Important: Members added to the group can modify, edit, and delete items. Please ensure that you only add members you trust.

Once a member is added, they’ll be able to access and edit everything in that shared group.

🔗 Need help finding your QR code to share with others? Check out How to find your profile’s QR Code.
