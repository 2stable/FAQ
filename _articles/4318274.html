You can make unlocking the Authenticator App even faster by using your Apple Watch—no need to type your password every time. To use this feature, you’ll first need to have Auto-Lock set up on your Mac.

To enable Unlock with Apple Watch:

  1. Open and unlock the Authenticator App on your Mac.

  2. Click the Settings section in the top-left corner.

  3. Find the Unlock with Apple Watch option.

  4. Toggle it ON.

Once enabled, you can unlock the Authenticator App on your Mac with your Apple Watch, quick and secure.
