A password manager is a secure app that helps you store and manage all your passwords in one place. Instead of remembering every password, you only need to remember one master password to access everything.

2Stable’s Password Manager is designed to make your digital life easier and more secure. You can:

  • Save unlimited logins, documents, and 2FA codes

  • Organize your accounts with folders and customizable fields

  • Auto-fill login details quickly on websites and apps

  • Generate strong, unique passwords with one tap

  • Share passwords securely with family or team members using Shared Groups

Everything you store is fully encrypted and backed up with iCloud, so only you—and the people you choose—can access it.
