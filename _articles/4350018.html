Adding your accounts to Password Manager is quick and easy, and you can customize each item to store everything you need, from login details to backup codes.

Step 1: Add a New Login

  1. Open the Password Manager app.

  2. Tap the + (plus) icon, either from the main screen or inside a folder.

  3. Tap Login.

  4. Choose a service (like Google), or create a custom one.

  5. Enter your username and password.

Step 2: Set Up Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

If your online account supports 2FA:

  • Scroll to the Two-Factor Authentication section in the item.

  • Tap the scan icon to scan a QR code, or enter the secret key manually.

  • A 6-digit code will then be generated in the app each time you log in.

Step 3: Save Backup or Recovery Codes

Some services give you backup codes when setting up 2FA. These are important in case you ever lose access to your 2FA device. To save them:

  1. Tap Add section.

  2. Tap Add field and choose Note.

  3. Paste your backup or recovery codes into the field.

  4. Tap the Section name and name it something like Backup Codes or Recovery Info.

As you can see, you can personalize your login item to suit your needs, add more sections or fields for things like security questions, support contact info, or custom notes.
